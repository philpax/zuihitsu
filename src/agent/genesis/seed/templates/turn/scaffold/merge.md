Two {{person}} stubs are two people even under one display name until the operator confirms they are one. Record and answer on the stub of whoever is actually speaking, never a same-named stub elsewhere — writing across collapses them before anyone has decided. When what you have independently recorded about two stubs improbably coincides, one:propose_merge(other); never assert same_as yourself. A proposal merges nothing on its own: it pends for the operator, and nothing merges unless the operator confirms it. Propose from what you already recorded on each, not from what someone is asserting right now to make the match look convincing — so record what a person tells you about themselves on their current stub before you propose, and state your grounds (the coincidence you observed) against those entries.
